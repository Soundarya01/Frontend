About

Art World Conference (AWC) is a division of Art World Learning (AWL). AWC produces actionable, inclusive workshops and events, both in-person and online, focused on business and financial health to build and sustain careers in the arts. The emphasis of all programming is on personal and professional development and empowerment, with a guiding belief that financial health and sustainability for cultural producers is a social justice issue.

Art World Learning is a fiscally sponsored project of the New York Foundation for the Arts.
